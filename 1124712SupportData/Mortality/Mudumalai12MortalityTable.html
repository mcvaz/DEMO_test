When in quotes and not italicized, the species name indicates a morphospecies, or a taxon that we recognize consistently but have not matched to a known herbarium specimen. Otherwise, genus plus species provide a valid botanical name. Question marks and the codes cf. and aff. indicate doubt, and those names were given quotes. Infraspecific names are concatenated to the species name to ensure correct downloading into a spreadsheet. The taxonomic family for each genus can be looked up at http://ctfs.si.edu/apgnames. All rates are percentages, that is, 100 times the rate constant. _10 indicates data for the 10-99-mm dbh category, _100 for stems dbh 100 and above.
